Alien Invasion War

Strange alien ships appear over the skies and cause heavy casualties. After the appearance of these ships, different aliens was descending from these ships. After a 24 hours, they conquered the city hall, a city proper, and an oil rig. To control the invasion, government decided to close thus places. After a week of battle between the military personnel and aliens, the government discovered that these aliens’ creature was building a tower that linked together in order to contact their allied forces and open a portal for their mothership.

Synopsis: This game was based on the ongoing Kdrama Movie Entitled “Duty After School”
